üìú Official League Regulations

‚è∞ 1. The Deadline

  • Predictions are strictly locked the exact minute that Practice 1 (P1) begins on Friday (or Sprint Qualifying on Sprint weekends).
  • Once cars are on track, no changes can be made.

üèéÔ∏è 2. Driver Scoring (P1, P2, P3, P11, P12, P19, P20)

  • Baseline: Everyone starts the race at 0 points.
  • Exact Match Bonus: If you guess the driver's exact finishing position, you get +2 Points.
  • Miss Penalty: You lose -1 Point for every position your prediction is away from the actual result.

üí• 3. The DNF Rule (Did Not Finish)

  • If a driver retires, crashes, or fails to finish the race (DNF), they are automatically scored as finishing in P20 for our math.

üõ†Ô∏è 4. Constructor Scoring (1st, 2nd, 5th, 6th, 10th)

  • Constructor ranks are based on the combined finishing positions of their two cars in that specific race.
  • Exact Match Bonus: +2 Points. Miss Penalty: -1 Point for every rank off.

üÉè 5. Wildcards & Penalties

  • Biggest Race Loser: Correctly guessing the driver who drops the most positions from grid to finish earns +5 Points.
  • Sprint Weekends: On Sprint weekends, two extra wildcards activate: Sprint Gainer and Sprint Loser (+5 Points each).
  • Missed Race: You receive the lowest score of the week, minus an additional 5 points.
